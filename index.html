About Me

Zachary Hilliard recieved his Ph.D. in Mathematics from Washington State University in 2020 under the direction of Prof. Lynn Schreyer. Previously he recieved his B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Washington State University in 2014.

Zachary's research interests include partial differential equations (PDEs), finite element methods (FEM), and nonlinear optimization. Topics such as the Cahn-Hilliard equation and other gradient descent and phase-field models are of particular interest.

Currently, Zachary is a postdoc at Oregon State University.
